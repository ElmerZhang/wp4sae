== Description ==

"无觅相关文章插件"是无觅网的核心产品, 它可以在短时间内提升您博客的访问量. 它会自动抓取你博客的内容, 然后以缩略图的形式展示最相关的文章. 其中的算法是基于用户行为, 文章内容, 文章标签, 时间以及大量相关参数去优化文章的点击率. 更重要的是, 一旦你的博客使用了"无觅相关文章插件", 你的博客将会自动整合到无觅网, 届时你的文章将会让更多的读者看到. 此插件主要针对的是中文博客. 详情请查看: http://www.wumii.com/widget/relatedItems.htm.

<h4>功能</h4>
* 支持视频截图.
* 在RSS显示有图片的相关文章, 带来更多流量.
* 一系列的自定义设置，包括显示位置、显示模式、文章数目、标题、外观样式等等.
* 更多强大的功能可在安装后前往无觅网站管理中心查看.


"Wumii Releated Posts" is the core product developed and maintained by wumii.com to quickly increase your blog's page views within a very short time. It indexed your site and automatically finds out related posts together with thumbnails. The algorithm is based on user behavior, article content, tags, time and a bunch of other parameters to optimize for click-through-rate. More than that, blogs using "Wumii Related Posts" will automatically integrate with wumii.com, and have their articles spread out to more readers. Again this is mainly targeted for Chinese blogs. For more information please visit: http://www.wumii.com/widget/relatedItems.htm.

<h4>Features</h4>
* Support video thumbnails (Chinese video sites only).
* Display related posts with thumbnails in rss feed and bring you more traffic.
* Highly customizable, including post location, style, title, display mode and a lot more.
* New features keep coming out, visit our site for details.

== Installation ==

1. 下载wumii-related-posts.zip.
1. 从插件管理界面上传插件.
1. 点击`现在安装`.
1. 在后台管理面板里的`设置` -> `无觅`可以找到插件的设置选项.

或

1. 解压缩下载的wumii-related-posts.zip.
1. 将解压缩后得到的文件夹上传到WordPress `/wp-content/plugins/` 目录下.
1. 在WordPress的后台`管理插件`页面启用`无觅相关文章插件`.


1. Download wumii-related-posts.zip.
1. Upload the zipped file directly in wordpress admin page.
1. Click `Install Now`.

OR

1. Download wumii-related-posts.zip and unzip it.
1. Copy the unzipped folder `wumii-related-posts` to `/wp-content/plugins/`
1. Activate the plugin.
1. Go to `Settings -> 无觅` and customize the options you want.
